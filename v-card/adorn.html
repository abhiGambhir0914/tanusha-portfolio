02

USER RESEARCH

Insights

  • Local vendors who produce handmade products tend to just sell the products in local markets.
  • Certain vendors don't have base money to invest in shops.
  • The work they produce is high end since all the products are of their ideation and handmade which adds more value to them.
  • But there less or lack of knowledge about promoting and putting products online on these platforms end up in not engaging these people as sellers.
  • What also happens is that since these people have the difficulty of putting their products online so what mediators do is they buy their products at small prices and then, launch those same products on online platforms for higher pricing.
  • One major problem that these people face is, all the steps involved in creating a seller account. Since they don't have an idea to fill up the description of product details, all these steps end up being time-consuming as well.
